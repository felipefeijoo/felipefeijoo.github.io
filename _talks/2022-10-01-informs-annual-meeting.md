Over 20 papers presented at the INFORMS Annual Meeting between 2011 and 2022. Topics span energy planning, bilevel optimization, cap-and-trade policy design, microgrid operation, and natural gas infrastructure modeling.

Felipe served as **Cluster Chair of the Energy Cluster** within the Energy, Natural Resources and the Environment (ENRE) section at INFORMS in 2018 and 2019, and as **INFORMS Student Chapter Advisor (Summa Cum Laude, 2025)**.
