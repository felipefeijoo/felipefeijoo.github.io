Graduate-level courses taught at PUCV on the mathematical foundations of operations research and their applications to energy and environmental systems.

* **Advanced Analytics and Machine Learning** – data-driven methods for industrial engineering and energy applications.
* **Stochastic Models** – stochastic programming, Markov chains, and decision-making under uncertainty.
* **Game Theory and Complementarity Optimization** – equilibrium models, bilevel and Stackelberg games applied to electricity markets and energy policy.
* **Energy Planning** – long-term capacity expansion, operational planning, and the role of Power-to-X and flexibility technologies.
